CONSUMER FIRST:
Collaboration

Working as one PMI team by sharing knowledge, skills and experience with others across markets, functions and teams to achieve shared goals

Positive Behaviours
  • Fosters a good working atmosphere.
  • Helps colleagues when noticing they are in need.
  • Shares less enjoyable duties to ensure there is good balance in the team tasks.
  • Shares success with others in the team, and works for a common goal.
  • Provides constructive feedback to allow colleagues’ professional development.

Development Tips for Improving - COLLABORATION

As per the individual skills gap report, try the following:

  • Participating – even passively – in team discussions (physical or remote), and pay attention to which information your colleagues may benefit from.
  • Create spaces or opportunities to share your knowledge or experience with your colleagues and support them wherever possible.
