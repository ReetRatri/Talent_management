EMPOWERING PEOPLE:
Communication

Providing context, clear direction and measurable objectives that is impactful and understandable to others

Positive Behaviours
  • Communicates information in a simple, timely way.
  • Is inquisitive ; asks the right questions to explore customer’s situation or needs.
  • Gives customers space to express opinions about service or products.
  • Shows deep understanding of customers’ needs.
  • Weights words and carefully choses them.
  • Takes the right time to explore customer’s needs.

Development Tips for Improving – COMMUNICATION

As per the individual skills gap report, try the following:

  • Schedule moments face to face with each of their colleagues to discuss and clarify the expectations you may have of each other.
  • Following up regularly on whether each party is meeting these expectations.
