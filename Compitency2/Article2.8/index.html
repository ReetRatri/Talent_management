Category Management

RRP and CC cat. understanding and CSM, Ranging (Platforms/devices, consumables/CC, accessories), Visual Merchandising (Shelving, planogramming, managing visibility of stock, setting and managing POS material, ensuring audit and compliance with visual merchandising standards)

Positive Behaviours
  • Is aware and respects the visual standards of the company.
  • Participate actively to the correct display of products.
  • Coordinates with customers to always ensure correct stock displaying.

Development Tips (As per Individual skills gap report)

Try the following:

  • Ask clients what they like and dislike about your competitors' product or service functionality/features and why they chose your product.
  • In order to gather more information, you can perform web searches to find out who the competitors are and explore their websites to learn about their offerings and position in the market.
  • Think about conducting and practicing SWOT analysis to evaluate each of your organization's competitors.
  • Reading industry publications may help you understanding new trends within the industry and the need to adapt existing products/services or introduce new ones.
  • Review existing sales proposals to observe how others have used competitive knowledge in the past.
